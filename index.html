About Jeff

Two decades at the intersection of security and leadership.

I'm a cybersecurity executive and digital leadership advisor with deep experience running security programs for complex organizations. I've sat in the CISO chair, navigated board-level conversations, and built teams that deliver real results — not just compliance checkboxes.

As an IANS Faculty member, I advise security leaders across industries on strategy, talent, and navigating the decisions that don't have easy answers.

My work centers on one belief: security is a business discipline, not just a technical one. The leaders who get that right are the ones who build lasting programs.
